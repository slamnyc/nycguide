Nightlife


Comedy Show
Come out to NYC's comedy clubs for a chance to enjoy a show from famous comedians and up-and-coming stars.
Quiet Clubbing
Put on headphones and dance the night away to some of the greatest hits!
Beer Garden
Enjoy a frosty beer on a summer day at any one the city's many beer gardens.
Hookah
Choose a flavor and have a chill night with your friends at a hookah lounge.

Rooftops
Head over to a rooftop bar while the weather is warm and get a view of the city from above.
Poetry Slam
Take part in judging a poetry competition at Nuyorican Poets Cafe.
Karaoke
Whether on stage or in a private room, there are many places for you to enjoy a night of karaoke in NYC.
Booze Cruise
Check out some of NYC's best booze cruises that will take you around the city for some spectacular views.
