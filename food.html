Places to Eat


Brooklyn Bagel | Manhattan
Tuscan artichoke on an everything bagel, plus many other tasty options. These bagels are unlike any others!
Queen's Comfort
Get your comfort on at this Astoria brunch spot where Southern dishes are served in a cool atmosphere. Make sure to get in line early!
Jing Fong
Get the traditional dim sum experience at Jing Fong where carts are constantly passing by to offer you the tastiest Hong Kong dishes.
Mama's Empanadas
With over a hundred of empanada options, from the traditional beef to peanut butter nutella, you are guaranteed to enjoy Mama's Empanadas.

Artichoke Pizza
Try out the thick, rich Artichoke pizza after a night out!
El Rey Del Taco
Between 6pm to 3am, swing by El Rey Del Taco truck to get their tasty tacos. I recommend the chorizo taco!
King Falafel
Vendy Award winner King Falafel will not disappoint. Catch them at their new restaurant location or at their truck. The chicken platter is recommended!
New Wonjo
Treat yourself to some authentic Korean BBQ at new Wonjo, located in the heart of Korean Town.

Taverna Kyclades
Octopodi, horiatiki, souvlaki - add Greek to your list of types of food to try and stop by Taverna Kyclades.
Calle Dao
A truly unique blend of Cuban Chinese cuisines. From the food to the d√©cor, the experience at Calle Dao will be enjoyable and unforgettable.
Toloache
Adventurous eaters should go to Toloache to try the chapulines tacos which consists of Oaxacan-style dried grasshoppers!
Butcher Bar
In the mood for barbecue food? Check out Butcher Bar!

Lady M
Lady M is known for its fresh and fine handmade layered cakes that come in various flavors. Be ready to wait in line.
Van Leeuwen
Earl Grey Ice Cream at Van Leeuwen is a must have, as well as other gourmet ice cream options.
Spot Dessert
For those with a sweet tooth, you'll really enjoy the blend of Asian and American flavors at Spot Dessert Bar.
10Below
Ever had ice cream rolls? Probably not. Check out 10Below, an ice cream parlor that is becoming increasingly popular in NYC.
